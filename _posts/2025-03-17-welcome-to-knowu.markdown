**Discover Who You Truly Are. Understand Others. Grow Together.**  

Welcome to KnowU! We are thrilled to introduce a multi-dimensional personality-insights platform designed for personal self-discovery, coaching, and team development. 

Rather than relying on a single static questionnaire, KnowU triangulates data from multiple sources to build a comprehensive, evidence-based portrait of your personality.

## What is KnowU?  
KnowU is a cutting-edge tool for **self-awareness, personal growth, and relationship insights**. We combine self-reflection with real-world inputs to map your personality across **15 traits** grounded in three established psychological models: **Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)**, **DISC**, and the **Big Five (Agreeableness)**.

## Key Features of KnowU

### 1. Triangulated Self-Evaluation
Complete our main card-swipe questionnaire to get your base profile, then enhance it by pasting social media posts or text feedback from friends for the AI to analyze. You can also take specialized assessments like *Leadership Material*.

### 2. Trait Self-Reflection
Alongside AI assessments, take control of your growth by setting personal self-scores (1–10 sliders) and writing reflection notes for any trait, allowing you to compare your self-image directly with AI and peer evaluations.

### 3. The Moral Compass
Engage with daily ethical dilemmas in a Tinder-style swipe interface. Discover what your binary choices (agree, oppose, or skip) reveal about your traits, and compare your decisions with community and friends' statistics.

### 4. Peer Feedback & Social Games
* **Feedback Requests**: Ask friends or colleagues specific questions (e.g., about punctuality or communication). Responses are scored 1–10, commented on, and automatically categorized by personality trait.
* **Blind Reveal**: Host group honesty games where anonymous feedback is hidden until all participants respond, then revealed simultaneously.
* **Who's Most Likely**: Vote within a group on fun statements and crown winners with trophies based on ranked voting.

### 5. Ask AI (Personality-Aware Chat)
Interact with a confidential chatbot that possesses full context of your profile, self-reflections, peer feedback, and moral decisions to receive highly personalized growth insights.

### 6. Teams & GROW Coaching
Managers and HR leaders can leverage KnowU to run structured GROW (Goal, Reality, Options, Will) coaching conversations, view team dynamics maps, and proactively prevent workplace friction using Friedrich Glasl's conflict escalation model.
