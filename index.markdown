<h2>What is KnowU?</h2>
<p>KnowU is a comprehensive tool for personal and professional growth, combining self-assessment, peer feedback,
    and social media insights to help you understand and enhance your personality and relationships.</p>

<h3>Key Features:</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Personality Assessment:</strong> Based on Myers-Briggs, DISC, and Big Five models</li>
    <li><strong>Peer Feedback:</strong> Anonymous evaluations from friends and colleagues</li>
    <li><strong>Social Media Insights:</strong> Understand your digital personality (upcoming)</li>
    <li><strong>Personal Growth Tracking:</strong> Monitor your development over time</li>
</ul>
