<h2>What is KnowU?</h2>
<p>KnowU is a personality-insights app that helps you discover, understand, and reflect on who you are through multiple lenses. Rather than relying on a single questionnaire, KnowU triangulates personality data from <strong>self-assessments</strong>, <strong>moral decision-making</strong>, <strong>peer observations</strong>, <strong>social media behaviour</strong>, and <strong>AI-powered conversations</strong> — all grounded in three established psychological frameworks.</p>

<h2>Personality Frameworks</h2>
<p>KnowU evaluates you across <strong>15 traits</strong> spanning three established psychological models:</p>

<div class="framework-cards">
  <div class="framework-card framework-card--disc">
    <h3>DISC</h3>
    <p class="framework-card__count">4 traits</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Dominance</strong> — Drive &amp; assertiveness</li>
      <li><strong>Influence</strong> — Sociability &amp; persuasion</li>
      <li><strong>Steadiness</strong> — Patience &amp; reliability</li>
      <li><strong>Compliance</strong> — Accuracy &amp; systematic thinking</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="framework-card framework-card--mbti">
    <h3>MBTI</h3>
    <p class="framework-card__count">10 traits (5 dimension pairs)</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Extraversion ↔ Introversion</li>
      <li>Sensing ↔ Intuition</li>
      <li>Thinking ↔ Feeling</li>
      <li>Judging ↔ Perceiving</li>
      <li>Assertive ↔ Turbulent</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="framework-card framework-card--bigfive">
    <h3>Big Five</h3>
    <p class="framework-card__count">1 trait</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Agreeableness</strong> — Cooperation, empathy &amp; trust</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
