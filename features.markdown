KnowU combines multiple signals — self-assessments, moral decisions, peer feedback, social media analysis, and AI conversations — to build a rich, multi-dimensional personality portrait.

<hr class="section-divider" />

<h2 id="self-evaluation">Self-Evaluation</h2>

<div class="feature-row">
  <div class="feature-row__screenshots">
    <img src="/media/screenshots/self-evaluation-questionnaires.png" alt="Self-Evaluation — Questionnaires" loading="lazy" />

    <img src="/media/screenshots/self-evaluation-analysis.png" alt="AI Analyzing Your Profile" loading="lazy" />

  <div class="feature-row__text">
    <h3>Personality Questionnaires</h3>
    <p>KnowU supports <strong>multiple questionnaires</strong>, each containing Likert-scale questions (Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree). The <strong>default assessment</strong> uses a 3-step wizard:</p>
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Self-Assessment</strong> — Answer personality questions through an intuitive card-swipe interface with per-question thematic icons and emoji-labelled options</li>
      <li><strong>Peer Feedback</strong> (optional) — Paste text feedback from friends or colleagues for the AI to factor in</li>
      <li><strong>Social Insights</strong> (optional) — Paste social media posts for the AI to analyse communication style and behavioural patterns</li>
    </ol>
    <p>Additional focused questionnaires — like <em>Leadership Material</em> — explore specific dimensions and produce a lightweight AI-generated summary with an alignment score, highlights, and development areas.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="feature-row feature-row--reverse">
  <div class="feature-row__screenshots">
    <img src="/media/screenshots/self-evaluation-reflection.png" alt="Trait Self-Reflection" loading="lazy" />
  </div>
  <div class="feature-row__text">
    <h3>Trait Self-Reflection</h3>
    <p>Alongside AI evaluations, you can maintain <strong>your own self-assessment</strong> for each of the 15 personality traits:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Set a personal <strong>self-score</strong> (1–10 slider) for any trait</li>
      <li>Add a <strong>personal note</strong> (up to 500 characters) explaining your reasoning</li>
      <li>Compare your self-score against the AI-generated score in a side-by-side view</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Self-reflections are auto-saved and stored separately from AI evaluations, giving you a persistent self-image to track over time.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h2 id="moral-compass">Moral Compass</h2>

<div class="feature-row">
  <div class="feature-row__screenshots">
    <img src="/media/screenshots/moral-compass-stories.png" alt="Moral Compass — Stories" loading="lazy" />
    <img src="/media/screenshots/moral-compass-responses.png" alt="Moral Compass — Community Responses" loading="lazy" />
  </div>
  <div class="feature-row__text">
    <h3>Daily Ethical Dilemmas</h3>
    <p>The Moral Compass is a <strong>Tinder-style card game</strong> presenting ethical dilemmas. Swipe through stories and make binary decisions — agree, oppose, or skip. Each story is linked to a personality trait, contributing to your overall profile.</p>
    <p>After voting, see community statistics (YES vs. NO split) and how your friends answered. New dilemmas are sourced daily from Reddit and transformed by AI into neutral, PII-free scenarios with relevant trait tags.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h2 id="ask-friends">Ask Friends</h2>

<div class="feature-row feature-row--reverse">
  <div class="feature-row__screenshots">
    <img src="/media/screenshots/ask-friends.png" alt="Ask Friends — Feedback Request" loading="lazy" />
    <img src="/media/screenshots/friends.png" alt="Find Friends" loading="lazy" />
  </div>
  <div class="feature-row__text">
    <h3>Peer Feedback Requests</h3>
    <p>Create a custom question (e.g., "How punctual am I in meetings?") and send it to selected friends. Recipients respond with a <strong>score (1–10)</strong> and an optional comment. Each question is auto-tagged to a personality trait, so results are grouped by dimension.</p>
    <p>The feedback hub has four sub-tabs: <strong>Respond</strong> (pending requests), <strong>Sent</strong> (outgoing), <strong>Received</strong> (completed, grouped by trait), and <strong>Given</strong> (evaluations you've submitted).</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="feature-row">
  <div class="feature-row__screenshots">
    <img src="/media/screenshots/blind-reveal.png" alt="Blind Reveal" loading="lazy" />
    <img src="/media/screenshots/who-most-likely.png" alt="Who's Most Likely" loading="lazy" />
  </div>
  <div class="feature-row__text">
    <h3>Social Games</h3>
    <h4>Blind Reveal</h4>
    <p>A group honesty game — write a question, pick friends, and everyone submits an anonymous answer. <strong>All answers stay hidden</strong> until every participant responds, then reveal simultaneously. This creates a safe space for truly honest feedback.</p>
    <h4>Who's Most Likely</h4>
    <p>Write a "Who is most likely to…" statement and select participants. Everyone votes for one person from the group. Votes remain hidden until all are in, then results show a ranked list with vote counts and a trophy for the winner.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h2 id="analytics">Analytics & Profiles</h2>

<div class="feature-row">
  <div class="feature-row__screenshots">
    <img src="/media/screenshots/analytics-ratings.png" alt="Analytics — Trait Scores Bar Chart" loading="lazy" />
    <img src="/media/screenshots/analytics-evaluation.png" alt="Analytics — MBTI Type & DISC Profile" loading="lazy" />    <img src="/media/screenshots/analytics-landscape.png" alt="Personality Landscape Radar Chart" loading="lazy" />  </div>
  <div class="feature-row__text">
    <h3>Your Personality Dashboard</h3>
    <p>Once you complete the default questionnaire, KnowU generates a comprehensive personality profile containing:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>MBTI type</strong> with a descriptive explanation (e.g., "ENTJ — The Commander")</li>
      <li><strong>15 trait scores</strong> (0–100), each colour-coded by framework</li>
      <li><strong>Key strengths</strong> and <strong>growth areas</strong></li>
      <li><strong>Narrative summary</strong> — an overall personality portrait</li>
    </ul>
    <p>The dashboard visualises your data through horizontal bar charts, a radar chart "Personality Landscape", a DISC donut chart, and aggregate statistics (evaluations, verdicts, AI conversations).</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="feature-row feature-row--reverse">

    <p>The <strong>Traits</strong> view is an educational reference for all 15 personality traits, grouped by framework (DISC, MBTI, Big Five). Each entry includes:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>A trait icon and category badge</li>
      <li>Your score (if a profile exists)</li>
      <li>Detailed description of what the trait measures</li>
      <li><strong>Key Focus</strong> — the trait's primary dimension</li>
      <li><strong>Strengths</strong> — positive characteristics of high scorers</li>
      <li><strong>Keep in Mind</strong> — potential challenges or blind spots</li>
    </ul>
